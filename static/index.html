FORMALISER.NET

The best backend for your webforms

(with zero sign up or configuration required)

https://formaliser.net/send?to=your@emailaddress.com

Effortless setup

Simply add the URL to your form's action attribute, add your email address to the URL, and you're good to go!

<form action="https://formaliser.net/send?to=your@emailaddress.com" method="post">

if your email address was info@formaliser.net, it would like this:

<form action="https://formaliser.net/send?to=info@formaliser.net" method="post">

No sign up required

Simply pop the URL into your form's action attribute and you're good to go!

Minimal configuration

Just add your email address to the URL and you're ready to receive submissions!

Security and privacy

Powered by Spacemail, your submissions are encrypted and stored securely.

Built-in spam protection

Rate limiting and hidden honeypot fields help to keep your inbox clean.

Reply-to address

Don't start a new thread, just hit reply; submissions will automatically set the sender's email address as the reply-to address, so you can keep your conversations in one place.

FREE and open source

FORMALISER.NET is completely free to use - in what world must you pay to receive emails? Even better, the source code is available on GitHub so you can host your own instance if you want to.
